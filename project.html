Food Festival '24 The Grade 12 Science students organized an incredible Food Festival, bringing a variety of delicious treats to the table! From kottu, mushroom buns, and rotti to popcorn, weli thalapa, achcharu, masala tea, and one-shot drinks, there was something for everyone to enjoy. The event was filled with energy as students gathered to grab their favorite snacks, making the festival a buzzing hub of flavors and fun. This fundraising project was a massive success, combining great food with a great cause, and leaving behind an unforgettable experience.
