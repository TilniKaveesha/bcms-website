Introduction

The Science Society of Bandaranayake College has a long and proud history of fostering scientific curiosity, innovation, and academic excellence among students. Through its numerous initiatives, including workshops, exhibitions, and competitions, the society has consistently inspired young minds to explore the wonders of science. Its flagship event,Vishvabiyathra, serves as a grand platform to celebrate scientific achievements and bring together passionate students, teachers, and experts. With a strong commitment to knowledge-sharing and hands-on learning, the Science Society continues to shape future scientists, engineers, and innovators, making a lasting impact on the school and beyond.
